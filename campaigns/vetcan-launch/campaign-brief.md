## Campaign Pillars

### Pillar 1: The Problem (Week 1-2)

**Theme:** "Phone tag is stealing from your operations"

**Content Focus:**
- The hidden cost of unanswered calls
- Booking back-and-forth frustration
- No-show revenue loss
- Staff burnout from repetitive tasks

**Platforms:** LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Instagram

---

### Pillar 2: The Solution (Week 3-4)

**Theme:** "VetCan: AI operations layer for appointment-based clinics"

**Content Focus:**
- What VetCan is (and isn't)
- Core capabilities overview
- How it integrates with existing workflows
- Human-in-the-loop design

**Platforms:** All platforms

**Key Asset:** HeyGen explainer video (60-90s)

---

### Pillar 3: Proof and Results (Week 5-6)

**Theme:** "Operators using VetCan see results"

**Content Focus:**
- Generalized results (redacted)
- Feature-specific benefits
- Before/after comparisons
- Testimonials (with releases)

**Platforms:** LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube

**Key Asset:** HeyGen testimonial-style video (30-45s)

---

### Pillar 4: Call to Action (Week 7-8)

**Theme:** "Ready to get those hours back?"

**Content Focus:**
- Demo offer
- Low-friction CTA
- Limited-time incentive (if applicable)
- FAQ and objection handling

**Platforms:** All platforms (paid amplification on LinkedIn)

**Key Asset:** HeyGen CTA video (15-30s)

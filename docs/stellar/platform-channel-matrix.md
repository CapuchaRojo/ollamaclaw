# Platform Channel Matrix

## Purpose

This document defines how VetCan content adapts to each social media platform. Each platform has unique audience expectations, format requirements, and engagement patterns.

## Platform Overview

| Platform | Primary Audience | Content Type | Engagement Goal |
|----------|------------------|--------------|-----------------|
| LinkedIn | Clinic operators, service business owners, industry | Professional, educational | Demo requests, leads |
| X/Twitter | Tech, startup, VC | Punchy, timely | Awareness, shares |
| Instagram | Visual, lifestyle, local | Polished, aspirational | Brand affinity |
| TikTok | Younger, discovery | Authentic, entertaining | Viral reach |
| YouTube | Search-driven, how-to | Educational, evergreen | Subscriptions |

---

## Channel Specifications

### LinkedIn

**Audience:** Clinic operators, service business owners, industry partners

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Post length | 150-300 characters (feed), up to 3000 (articles) |
| Video | 15-90 seconds, 9:16 or 1:1 |
| Images | 1200x627 (landscape), 1080x1080 (square) |
| Hashtags | 3-5 relevant tags |
| Posting cadence | 3-5x per week |
| Best times | Tue-Thu, 8-10am or 12-1pm |

**Tone:** Professional, authoritative, value-driven

**Content types that work:**
- Thought leadership posts
- Product announcements
- Customer success stories (redacted)
- Industry insights
- Team/hiring posts

**Note:** Target audience is appointment-based service businesses (e.g., medical cannabis clinics, wellness centers, clinic operators, front desk teams). Veterinary clinics are a future vertical, not the current primary target.

**Example post structure:**
```
[Hook: Problem statement]
[Context: Why this matters]
[Solution: VetCan approach]
[Proof: Generalized result]
[CTA: Comment, DM, or demo link]
```

---

### X/Twitter

**Audience:** Tech community, startup ecosystem, VC, early adopters

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Post length | 280 characters (can thread) |
| Video | Up to 2:20, 16:9 or 9:16 |
| Images | 1600x900 (landscape), 1080x1080 (square) |
| Hashtags | 1-3 trending or branded |
| Posting cadence | 1-3x per day |
| Best times | Weekdays 12-3pm, evenings 7-9pm |

**Tone:** Punchy, timely, slightly irreverent

**Content types that work:**
- Product teasers
- Quick tips
- Industry commentary
- Thread explainers
- Meme-adjacent content (careful with brand)

**Example post structure:**
```
[Hook: Surprising stat or contrarian take]
[Setup: 1-2 context tweets]
[Solution: VetCan angle]
[CTA: Link or question]
```

---

### Instagram

**Audience:** Visual-first, lifestyle-oriented, local discovery

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Caption length | Up to 2200 characters (first 125 visible) |
| Video (Reels) | 15-90 seconds, 9:16 |
| Images | 1080x1350 (portrait), 1080x1080 (square) |
| Hashtags | 5-15 relevant tags |
| Posting cadence | 3-7x per week (feed), daily (stories) |
| Best times | Weekdays 11am-1pm, evenings 7-9pm |

**Tone:** Warm, aspirational, authentic

**Content types that work:**
- Behind-the-scenes
- Product in action (polished)
- Customer love (with releases)
- Team culture
- Educational carousels

**Example post structure:**
```
[Hook: Emotional or visual grab]
[Story: Relatable moment]
[Solution: VetCan role]
[Proof: Soft testimonial]
[CTA: Link in bio or DM]
```

---

### TikTok

**Audience:** Gen Z, Millennials, discovery-driven

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Caption length | 150 characters max |
| Video | 15-60 seconds, 9:16 |
| Hashtags | 3-5 trending + niche |
| Posting cadence | 1-4x per day |
| Best times | Weekdays 6-10am, evenings 7-11pm |

**Tone:** Authentic, entertaining, unpolished-feeling

**Content types that work:**
- Day-in-the-life
- Problem/solution skits
- Trending audio adaptations
- Quick tips
- "Things I wish I knew" format

**Example post structure:**
```
[Hook: 0-2s, visual or text overlay]
[Problem: Relatable pain point]
[Solution: Quick VetCan mention]
[Result: Satisfying outcome]
[CTA: Follow or link in bio]
```

---

### YouTube (Shorts + Long-form)

**Audience:** Search-driven, how-to learners

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Title | 60 characters max (Shorts), 100 (long-form) |
| Description | 5000 characters (first 150 critical) |
| Video (Shorts) | 15-60 seconds, 9:16 |
| Video (Long) | 5-15 minutes, 16:9 |
| Thumbnail | 1280x720 minimum |
| Posting cadence | 1-2x per week |
| Best times | Weekdays 2-4pm, weekends 9-11am |

**Tone:** Educational, authoritative, evergreen

**Content types that work:**
- Product tutorials
- Feature deep-dives
- Customer interviews (redacted)
- Industry explainers
- Webinar recordings

**Example post structure:**
```
[Title: Search-optimized + benefit]
[Hook: 0-15s, what they'll learn]
[Content: Structured, timestamped]
[CTA: Subscribe, link in description]
```

## Engagement and Response Rules

### DO

- Respond to comments within 24 hours
- Thank users for positive mentions
- Route product questions to DM or support
- Escalate complaints to internal team channel

### DO NOT

- Argue with negative commenters
- Make product promises in comments
- Share customer-specific info publicly
- Engage with trolls or bad-faith actors
